Bio

I'm a second-year, full-time Master of Urban Planning student concentrating on environmental and infrastructure planning. I grew up in Waldorf, Maryland but I'm now based in Long Island City, New York. Currently, I am a consultant Urban Planning Fellow for Manhattan Community Board 1, where I am conducting a streetscape survey and analysis of Lower Manhattan. My other recent experience includes the NYC Department of City Planning, the SoHo Broadway Initiative, and the Eagle Street Rooftop Farm. After completing my degree, I aim to work in NYC focusing on parks and open space planning. My personal interests include running, gardening, travel and yoga.
